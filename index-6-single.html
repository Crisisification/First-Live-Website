Common Questions &
Helpful Answers

Transparent Pricing
No hidden fees. We believe in clear, upfront costs for all our legal services.
Expert Guidance
Our team brings years of specialized experience in Corporate, Family, and Estate law.
  • How do I book a consultation?
    You can book directly through this website by clicking the Contact Us button, or give us a call at 416-572-5296. We offer both in-person and virtual appointments.
  • What areas of law do you specialize in?
    We specialize in Corporate Law, Real Estate Law, and Wills & Estates. Whether you are incorporating a business, buying a home, or planning your legacy, our team provides expert, personalized guidance.
  • What should I bring to my first meeting?
    Please bring a piece of valid government-issued ID and all relevant documents related to your case such as contracts, court orders, or financial statements. This helps us understand your situation immediately and provide accurate advice.
  • How does your pricing work?
    We believe in transparency. Depending on the service, we offer both flat-rate fees (common for Wills and Incorporations) and competitive hourly rates. We will always discuss and agree upon the fee structure before beginning any work.
